About The Artists & Platform

Mission, Vision, & Platform Overview:

Centered in Dallas, Texas, Beyond Photography is a platform, founded upon the doctrines of innovation, ingenuity, authenticity, resourcefulness, unadulterated creativity, purpose, and synergy, through which mediums of photography alongside analyses and distribution of such is facilitated to an international audience. It aims to provide audiences with a multisensory, multidisciplinary experience within and across photographic genres in natural, urban, and artistic photography and beyond across the southwestern United States. Through these objectives, a collaborative, creativity-centered, and project-driven environment is fostered while aiming to expand its scope to a broader scope to distribute snapshots of digital artistry for individuals of all walks of life.

About The Artist:

Joon Lee is an intermediate contemporary digital artist, photographer, and student, his developing portfolio and distinctive manipulation of photographic elements in symbolically conveying differing mediums of graphic communications, elevating them from mere photographs to surreal, lifelike experiences. His passions for technological niches and subsets, international travel, and Asian familial roots have amalgamated to forge a compelling, up-and-rising, and future-centered life narrative. "Photography Is the Story I Fail to Put Into Words." - Destin Sparks "Photography Is an Art of Teleporting the Past Into the Future." - Mehmet Murat Ä°ldan

Story & Lifelong Influences of Photography:

From adolescence, photography has served as an outlet in which he could foster unadulterated creativity through its formulation of versatile, compelling narratives that have altered the face of his world outlook. Through snapshots of everyday phenomena alongside composition and editing of such, photography has metamorphosized from a hobby to a passion in amalgamation with his pre-existing passions for graphic communications, video production, technology and computer science, website development, and intercultural exposure through travel, all of which he has channeled through this medium of art. Through both academic and personal pursuits in these interrelated fields, he has developed an increased awareness of the growing impacts of technology, and, as the crossroad between such and the fine arts, has developed an unparalleled passion that has ultimately promoted the individual, and, in the course of time, collective development of this website in February of 2022 within and outside of classroom doors. In an increasingly interconnected world, the fine arts has progressively transitioned into its digital medium, yet, within this development, elevated the authenticity, self-expression, and universality that artwork promotes on an international level.
